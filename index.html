Welcome to the all new thirty under 40

These in-depth profiles spotlight the next generation of leaders who are shaping the oil and gas industry. Presented by our sponsor Grant Thornton, the 30 Under 40 honorees represent the best of the young professionals in the E&P, Service, A&D, Midstream and Finance sectors. From a field of hundreds of nominees, Oil and Gas Investor has selected 30 noteworthy individuals who possess the intelligence, creativity, community involvement and values that will propel the industry toward a bright future.
